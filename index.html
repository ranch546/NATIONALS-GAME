HOW TO PLAY

90 questions · Level 1 Easy (30) → Level 2 Medium (30) → Level 3 Hard (30)

▶ THE QUIZ

  • Tap START QUIZ — full run is 90 questions
  • Level 1 · Easy — first 30 easy 1980s questions (in order)
  • Level 2 · Medium — next 30 medium questions
  • Level 3 · Hard — last 30 hard questions
  • 15 seconds each (Time Boost upgrade adds more)
  • Tap a card or press 1–4 on your keyboard

📊 SCORING

  • +25 points per correct answer
  • −10 points per wrong answer
  • +50 XP per correct answer (XP upgrades boost this)
  • Finish Easy or Medium with a negative score → level fails, run ends
  • Need 0+ points at the end of each tier to keep going

⭐ XP & LEVELS

  • Earn +50 XP every time you get a question right
  • 400 XP fills your profile bar — then you level up
  • Level up → +15 🪙 coins added to your wallet
  • XP saves to your profile — keeps growing across quiz runs
  • Buy the XP Boost upgrade in the shop for bonus XP

♥ LIVES

  • Start with 3 hearts — hit 0 and it's game over
  • Wrong answer → −1 ♥
  • Earn +1 ♥ for a perfect level (all correct in Easy, Medium, or Hard)
  • Earn +1 ♥ by winning the bonus mini-game

⚡ 50/50 LIFELINE

  • Need 2+ hearts — tap USE LIFELINE at the top
  • Costs 1 ♥ · removes 2 wrong answers
  • You get 10 seconds to pick from the 2 left
  • One lifeline per question

🎮 BONUS CHALLENGE

  • After a correct answer, tap BONUS CHALLENGE
  • Random mini-game (Reaction, Memory, Pac-Man, etc.)
  • Win → +1 ♥ · lose → no penalty, keep playing

🪙 COINS & SHOP

  • Earn +30 🪙 for finishing each quiz level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Earn +15 🪙 each time your profile levels up from XP
  • Spend coins on UPGRADES (time, coin boost, eliminator, lucky save, XP)
  • Check TROPHIES for 8 unlockable achievements
  • LEADERBOARD ranks every profile on this device
